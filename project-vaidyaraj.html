Vaidyaraj Online Store

This project is a comprehensive Online E-commerce website

designed to offer users a seamless and user-friendly experience for purchasing various products online. The site allows customers to create personal accounts, explore products options, and complete purchases securely through the integrated RazorPay payment gateway.


Key Features:

1. User Account Management:
  • User Registration & Login: Customers can easily register on the platform and securely log in to access their personal accounts.
  • Profile Management: Users can update personal information and track their policy purchases and payment history within their account dashboard.
2. Products Category:

The site offers a variety of Products Offerse, including:

  • Ayurvedic
  • Homeopathic
  • Combo Products
  • Popular Products

Prices for all policies vary depending on the customer's profile, age, and the specific plan selected.


3. RazorPay Payment Gateway:
  • Secure and reliable online payment processing integrated with RazorPay.
  • Customers can make payments using various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking.
5. Admin Portal:

A dedicated admin portal has been developed to manage the backend operations efficiently, providing full control over critical aspects of the business.

Key functionalities include:

  • Customer Purchase Details: View and manage all customer purchases, including policy types, amounts paid, and transaction history.
  • Site Management: A section to manage Customer details, roles, and responsibilities.
